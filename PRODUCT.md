# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Project managers leading small teams who need a simple, Jira-like workspace to organize tasks, assign ownership, and keep delivery moving without unnecessary complexity.

## Product Purpose
TaskFlow helps project leads plan, assign, and track work in one place. It exists to make day-to-day execution clear: who owns what, what is in progress, what is blocked or overdue, and what needs attention next.

## Brand Personality
Simple, efficient, clear. The interface should feel focused and calm, with straightforward language and low-friction flows that support fast decision-making.

## Anti-references
- Overloaded interfaces packed with too much information at once.
- Dense screens that force users to scan noisy metrics before they can act.
- Decorative UI that adds visual complexity without improving task execution.

## Design Principles
- Prioritize action clarity: users should immediately see ownership, status, and next steps.
- Keep cognitive load low: progressive disclosure over information dumps.
- Favor consistent, familiar interaction patterns over novelty.
- Optimize for daily flow: quick updates, minimal friction, predictable navigation.
- Make state visible and trustworthy: overdue, blocked, and completed work must be easy to distinguish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA across the product. Ensure accessible color contrast, keyboard-friendly interactions, clear focus states, readable content hierarchy, and reduced-motion-respectful behavior for animated UI.
